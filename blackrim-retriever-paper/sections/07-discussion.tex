\section{Discussion}
\label{sec:discussion}

\tothink{full discussion prose; outline below covers four sub-themes.}

\subsection{Where the conservative gate pays its conservatism}

\tothink{the gate forgoes aggregate NDCG@10 on cells where the
posterior on a candidate-arm-vs-baseline gap has not yet tightened
enough to admit. Bites hardest on novel query distributions
(consumer repos with traffic profiles unlike Blackrim's) where the
prior matrix is least informative.}

\subsection{The Bruch et al.\ open question: RRF vs.\ convex combination}

The fusion-functions analysis in Bruch et al.\ 2023
\cite{bruch2023analysis} found that convex combination of normalised
scores beats RRF on every BEIR dataset they tested when $\alpha$ is
tuned per-domain --- but RRF's calibration-free robustness wins on
out-of-domain data and under score-distribution shift. Our policy
treats this directly: hybrid-RRF and hybrid-CC are both arms in the
$\armset$, and the contextual-bandit policy can prefer one over the
other per query class. \tothink{report empirically which class
preferred RRF vs.\ CC, with what gap, and whether the CC preference
held across the trace or drifted.}

\subsection{When this won't work}

\tothink{three regimes break the architecture as currently described:
(i) \emph{very small corpora} ($< 50$ docs) where IDF is too noisy
even for BM25 --- the keyword fallback is the right default;
(ii) \emph{large-corpus consumer repos} where SPLADE's true value
emerges --- the gate threshold needs operator-tuning per consumer;
(iii) \emph{non-stationary corpora} where the optimal arm per class
drifts faster than the posterior can update --- discounted Thompson
sampling \cite{rajcoomar2017discounted} is the standard answer,
parking-lotted in \cref{sec:future}.}

\subsection{Lessons from deployment}

\tothink{three deployment lessons cross-applicable beyond this paper:
(i) per-class baselines as the safety constraint surface (not aggregate
"don't regress on average") --- this is the substantive methodological
point; (ii) corpus-size gates on neural components matter for
cold-start consumer repos; (iii) paper-stream telemetry is the
load-bearing observability surface --- without per-query records of
both per-scorer and final-fused ranks, the off-line evaluation pipeline
is impossible.}
